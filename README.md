# AI Travel Film

### Your travel videos → cinematic short film. No editing skills needed.

> **You don't need to know video editing.**  
> **You don't need to learn any software.**  
> **Just open Windsurf, tell it where your videos are, and say "make me a travel film."**

## How it works

1. **Download [Windsurf](https://windsurf.com)** — a free AI-powered code editor
2. **Open this project** in Windsurf
3. **Tell the AI what you want** — in plain language, like talking to a friend
4. **Get your video** — the AI handles everything automatically

That's it. No commands to memorize. No settings to configure. Just talk to the AI.

---

## Getting started

### Step 1: Install Windsurf

Go to [windsurf.com](https://windsurf.com) and download it. It's free.  
Install it like any normal app.

### Step 2: Open this project

- Download this project (green "Code" button → "Download ZIP")
- Unzip it anywhere on your computer
- In Windsurf, click **File → Open Folder** and select the unzipped folder

### Step 3: Tell the AI what you want

Click the **Cascade** chat panel on the right side of Windsurf, and type something like:

> **"My travel videos are in D:\Videos\summer-trip. Make me a 2-minute cinematic travel film."**

Or try any of these:

> "I have drone footage in ~/Videos/drone and GoPro clips in ~/Videos/gopro. Create a travel film with slow-motion highlights and background music."

> "Make a short vertical video for TikTok from my vacation videos in ~/Movies/bali"

> "我的旅行视频在 D:\旅行视频 文件夹里，帮我做一个2分钟的旅行短片，要有BGM和字幕"

The AI will:
- ✅ Check if ffmpeg is installed (and tell you how to install it if not)
- ✅ Scan your video folders
- ✅ Analyze every clip for motion, brightness, and quality
- ✅ Pick the best moments
- ✅ Arrange them into a cinematic story (opening → journey → climax → ending)
- ✅ Add smooth transitions, color grading, music, and subtitles
- ✅ Export a ready-to-publish video

### Step 4: Watch and refine

After the first version is done, you can say things like:

> "The opening is too slow, speed it up"

> "I want more drone shots in the beginning"

> "Can you add Chinese subtitles?"

> "Make it shorter, under 1 minute"

> "The music doesn't match, try something more upbeat"

The AI will adjust and rebuild. Each round takes about 10 minutes.

## Example prompts you can copy & paste

### Basic
```
My travel videos are in [YOUR FOLDER PATH].
Make me a 2-minute cinematic travel film with background music.
```

### With specific requests
```
My videos are in [YOUR FOLDER PATH].
I have drone footage, GoPro action cam clips, and 4K camera recordings.
Make a cinematic travel film with:
- An epic drone opening shot
- Fast-paced journey section
- Slow-motion highlights from the 4K footage
- Poetic subtitles
- Cinematic background music
```

### Vertical for social media
```
My trip videos are in [YOUR FOLDER PATH].
Make a 60-second vertical (9:16) travel reel for Instagram/TikTok.
```

```
帮我做一个2分钟的旅行短片：
- 开头用航拍大全景

- 高潮用慢动作
- 加上中文字幕和背景音乐
```

## Tips for better results

- **More footage = better film** — 10+ clips gives the AI more to choose from
- **Mixed camera types work great** — drone + action cam + phone = visual variety
- **High frame rate footage shines** — 120fps video becomes beautiful slow motion
- **Tell the AI your story** — "This was a cycling trip through the mountains" gives context
- **Iterate** — first version is a starting point, refine with follow-up prompts
